Beesechurger

A burger for those who dare to go bold

Only the strongest will survive.

Ingredients

  • 1lb of ground beef
  • 2 slices of American cheese
  • 1 set of upside down buns
  • 1 cup tomato reduction

Recipe instructions

  1. Cook beef
  2. Toast buns
  3. Spread tomato reduction on bottom, upside down bun
  4. Place cooked beef between buns, on top of tomato reduction
  5. Wrap buns with two slices of cheese, using hand warmth to melt the cheese
